Research Experience

Graduate Research Fellow, Sogin Lab
University of California Merced
2021 – Present

Developed cultivation-based approaches to target isolation of plant growth-promoting bacteria from Zostera marina rhizosphere, rhizoplane, and endosphere compartments (>400 isolates, 296 with assembled whole genome sequences from PacBio and Nanopore).
Applied computational metabolic modeling to design minimal bacterial consortia for seagrass restoration.
Conducted metagenomic, metatranscriptomic, and metabolomic analyses of seagrass rhizospheres.
Field collection of Zostera marina at UC Davis Bodega Marine Laboratory.
Designed and conducted raceway inoculation experiments testing plant growth-promoting bacterial consortia on seagrass performance.
