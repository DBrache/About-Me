A curated collection of tools, databases, and resources I use and recommend for microbial ecology, bioinformatics, science communication, and graduate school life.

Bioinformatics & Computational Tools

  • Learn R with swirl

    Integrated Microbial Genomes & Microbiomes — genome browsing, annotation, and comparative analysis.

  • The R GRaph Gallery

    Gallery of R graphs and plots using tidyverse and ggplot. It has a neat tool to visualize palettes

  • microViz

    Sooooo helpful for cleaning phyloseq taxanomy tables.

  • KBase

    Great for getting starting with bioinformatics or for those without access to high performance computing systems. There are many pipelines you can use with literature to back it up.
